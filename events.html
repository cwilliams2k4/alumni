ASU Cares

Homeless Children's Playtime Project

Saturday April 4
11 a.m. – 2 p.m.

This year in connection with the nationwide ASU Cares Month, the National Capital Chapter is partnering with the Homeless Children's Playtime Project to prepare their Spring Backpack Distribution. These backpacks are filled with school supplies and activity kits to help ensure children have enough supplies for the last portion of the school year. These bags are distributed in time for spring break when children are often in the shelter all day. Volunteers will be stuffing, sorting and boxing backpacks by shelter site and age.

Sign up here

Join our Facebook event here

In conjunction with this project, we are raising money to purchase books for these children through Usborne Books & More's Literacy for a Lifetime program. This program will match any amount we raise by 50%, once we hit $200! Our goal is to raise $500 which will in turn be $750 worth of books for the Homeless Children's Playtime Project! If you can't volunteer on April 4, please consider giving generously.

Location:
St. Stephens Church

Contact Nina Deal, if you have any questions.

CAN Softball is Coming Soon!

Every year we sponsor a softball team in the Capital Alumni Network Softball league. Practices will start later this month and games start in April. The season runs through the spring/summer ending in August at the annual CAN Softball Tournament in Greenbelt, MD!

Most games are held on weeknights around the National Mall with teams showing up at 6 p.m. There will be some weekend games as well.

If interested in participating this year, please email our head coach Jon Lyons.
